Kairo — Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 3, 2026

Overview

Kairo ("the App") is a focus timer app developed by Apex Digital. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data Kairo collects, how it's used, and your rights.

Kairo does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data stays on your device.

Data Collection

All information remains exclusively on your device:

  • Focus session data (duration, scores, timestamps) — stored locally using Core Data
  • Goals and preferences — stored locally using UserDefaults
  • Calendar events — created only with your explicit permission via Apple's EventKit, stored in your iOS Calendar
  • Motion data — used only for pickup detection during focus sessions, never stored or transmitted
  • Notification preferences — managed locally through iOS notification system

What We Don't Collect

✓ No account creation required
✓ No email addresses
✓ No names or personal identifiers
✓ No location data
✓ No usage analytics or telemetry
✓ No advertising identifiers
✓ No data shared with third parties
✓ No cloud sync or server communication

Permissions

Kairo may request the following permissions, all optional:

  • Notifications — To send focus reminders, session completion alerts, and streak notifications
  • Calendar Access — To schedule focus sessions on your iOS Calendar and set reminders
  • Motion & Fitness — To detect phone pickups during focus sessions (distraction tracking)

You can revoke any permission at any time through iOS Settings.

Data Storage

All data is stored exclusively on your device. If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed. There is no cloud backup of Kairo data.

Children's Privacy

Kairo does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app contains no age-restricted content and does not require any personal information to use.

Third-Party Services

Kairo uses no third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising frameworks. The app is entirely self-contained.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
